Muhammad Ayoub

School of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiaotong University, China

I am an Associate Professor of Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I do some research on pervasive learning in e-Learning lab. I started affective computing research when I visited Prof. Victor Callaghan, University of Essex in 2006 where our affective Learning work was reported by New Scientist. I visited Prof. Jean Bacon of Computer Laboratory, University of Cambridge in 2010. I am also on the board of Chinese e-Learning Technology Standardization Committee since 2004, and the project co-editor of ISO/IEC 12785-1:2009 and other projects of ISO/IEC JTC1 SC36 since 2008.
